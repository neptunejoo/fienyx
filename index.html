Core Service

Translation

Every translation we produce is guided by two uncompromising ideals: faithfulness and felicity. Faithfulness means the source meaning is rendered accurately, nothing added, nothing lost. Felicity means the result reads naturally in the target language, as if it were written there to begin with.

Every translation is also culturally adapted, localized for the right demographics, context, and subject matter, so your message connects rather than just converts.

Request a Quote
  • Academic and scientific documents
  • Legal and official materials
  • Business and corporate content
  • Published books and manuscripts
  • Advertising and marketing materials
  • Culturally adapted localization
  • Subject-matter expertise across fields
2026 Standard

Intelligent Transcription

This is not simple dictation. It is Intelligent Data Capture. We deliver time-stamped, speaker-identified transcripts built for your specific use case: SEO optimization, database ingestion, or accessibility compliance.

Where AI-generated transcripts hallucinate, our human-audited process guarantees verified accuracy. For legal depositions, medical records, or academic research, that difference is everything.

Request a Quote
  • Time-stamped, speaker-identified transcripts
  • Multi-format output: JSON, XML, plain text
  • SRT/VTT subtitle and caption files
  • Audio and video files (.mp4, .wav, .mp3, live links)
  • SEO-optimized transcript formatting
  • Legal and medical verified accuracy
  • Human-audit for AI hallucination removal
Editing Service

Light Editing

A targeted pass through your text that catches surface-level errors and smooths rough edges, all while preserving your original voice and structure. Ideal for writing that is nearly there but needs a final push toward clarity and correctness.

Request a Quote
  • Grammar, spelling, and punctuation
  • Minor syntax errors
  • Confusing or inconsistent word and phrase usage (with comments)
  • Errors in common facts and general knowledge
  • Words inappropriate for the subject or intended audience
  • Guidance on quotation permissions and citations
Substantive Editing

Heavy Editing

A comprehensive structural and linguistic overhaul examining cohesion, coherence, parallelism, and overall clarity from the ground up. We strengthen logical flow, eliminate redundancy, and tailor your content for maximum cultural relevance to your target audience.

We distinguish between Technical Editing (precision and clarity) and Creative Editing (tone and style), and apply whichever or both your project demands.

Request a Quote
  • Grammar, spelling, capitalization, format, and punctuation
  • Language that hinders effective communication
  • Illogical flow, repetition, and contradictory content
  • Titles, illustrations, tables, numbers, and references
  • Cross-cultural optimization for target demographics
  • Rhetoric and figurative language choices
  • Pronouns and connectors for smooth, continuous reading
Linguistic QA

Zero-Error Proofreading

A multi-pass Linguistic Quality Assurance audit, far more than a spell-check. We verify grammar, formatting consistency, and brand-voice alignment to certify that your document is truly publication-ready.

Think of it as the final safety net for high-stakes campaigns, academic submissions, and any publication where a single error can cost credibility.

Request a Quote
  • Multi-pass zero-error audit
  • Spelling errors and typos
  • Capitalization and punctuation
  • Formatting: font size, bold/italic, spacing, indentation
  • Brand-voice alignment check
  • Formatting consistency throughout the document
  • Minor oversights from the writer or editor
