Core Service

Translation

We abide by the ideals of faithfulness and felicity in all translations. Faithfulness ensures the accurate rendering of source meaning. Felicity ensures the translated text reads naturally to native speakers.

Our translations are inherently adapted to their target audience — localized for demographics, culture, and subject matter.

Request a Quote
  • Academic and scientific documents
  • Legal and official materials
  • Business and corporate content
  • Published books and manuscripts
  • Advertising and marketing materials
  • Culturally adapted localization
  • Subject-matter expertise across fields
2026 Standard

Intelligent Transcription

Not just typing — Intelligent Data Capture. We deliver time-stamped, speaker-identified transcripts built for your specific use case: SEO optimization, database ingestion, or accessibility compliance.

Where AI-generated transcripts hallucinate, our human-audited process delivers verified accuracy. Critical for legal and medical contexts where inaccuracies carry liability.

Request a Quote
  • Time-stamped, speaker-identified transcripts
  • Multi-format output: JSON, XML, plain text
  • SRT/VTT subtitle and caption files
  • Audio and video files (.mp4, .wav, .mp3, live links)
  • SEO-optimized transcript formatting
  • Legal and medical verified accuracy
  • Human-audit for AI hallucination removal
Editing Service

Light Editing

A focused pass on your text to correct surface-level errors while preserving your voice and structure. Ideal for texts that need polishing but not a full structural overhaul.

Request a Quote
  • Grammar, spelling, and punctuation
  • Minor syntax errors
  • Confusing or irregular word/phrase usage (with comments)
  • Errors in common facts and knowledge
  • Inappropriate words for the subject and readership
  • Guidance on quotation permissions and citations
Substantive Editing

Heavy Editing

A comprehensive structural and linguistic revision — examining cohesion, coherence, parallelism, and overall clarity. Enhanced for impact, logical flow, and cultural relevance to your specific target audience.

We differentiate between Technical Editing (precision and clarity) and Creative Editing (tone and style).

Request a Quote
  • Grammar, spelling, capitalization, format, and punctuation
  • Language that affects effective communication
  • Illogical flow, repetitiveness, contradictory content
  • Titles, illustrations, tables, numbers, and references
  • Cross-cultural optimization for target demographics
  • Rhetoric and figure of speech choices
  • Pronouns and conjunctions for continuous flow
Linguistic QA

Zero-Error Proofreading

A multi-pass Linguistic Quality Assurance audit — not just typo-checking. We verify grammar, formatting consistency, and brand-voice alignment to ensure your text is publication-ready.

The "insurance policy" for marketing campaigns, high-stakes publications, and academic submissions where a single error carries consequence.

Request a Quote
  • Multi-pass zero-error audit
  • Spelling errors and typos
  • Capitalization and punctuation
  • Formatting: word size, bold/italic, spacing, indentation
  • Brand-voice alignment check
  • Formatting consistency across document
  • Minor writer/editor oversights
